Blockchain Technology Explained

Blockchain is a digital ledger technology that records transactions securely across multiple computers. It is the core technology behind cryptocurrencies and many modern decentralized applications.

How Blockchain Works

Transactions are grouped into blocks and linked together in a chain. Each block contains a unique cryptographic hash that secures the information.

Security Benefits

Because blockchain data is distributed across many nodes, it is extremely difficult to modify or hack the system.

Applications of Blockchain

Blockchain technology is used in finance, supply chain management, digital identity systems, and many other industries.
